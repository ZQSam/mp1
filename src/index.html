About Me

Academic and Professional Journey

I was born in Wenzhou, China, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics-Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). My academic journey has been enriched through coursework in machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and algorithm design.
I have gained hands-on experience as a research assistant, working on enhancing the robustness of transformer-based motion planning algorithms. This research integrates autonomous navigation techniques and state-of-the-art neural network frameworks to address real-world challenges.
My professional experiences include internships where I developed data-driven marketing platforms, leveraging tools such as Java, Spring Boot, and Flink SQL to optimize ad strategies. In addition, I actively participate in leadership roles, serving as the head of advertising for UCSD's Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CSSA).
I am passionate about advancing AI and machine learning frameworks to develop scalable, accessible solutions, particularly in the fields of autonomous systems and personalized education platforms.
